Hanni Pham reviewed on: January 20, 2024

Okay, first of all, let me just say WOW Le Sserafim really came in HOT with their debut album! UNFORGIVEN is like a chaotic party where every genre got an invite: Western showdowns ("Unforgiven"), tropical beach vibes ("No-Return"), even some chill melancholic moments ("Blue Flame"). It's all over the place, but somehow... it kinda works?

The title track "Unforgiven" is a total banger, ngl. Sass level with that whole cowboy concept. And "Antifragile" is basically their anthem now, right? That chorus gets stuck in your head like crazy, but in a good way!

But hold up, not everything's perfect. The album kinda jumps around between styles, which can be fun, but also feels a bit messy at times. Some songs like "Fearnot" and "Flash Forward" are alright, but kinda forgettable compared to the powerhouses up front.

Overall, UNFORGIVEN is a solid debut with tons of potential. Le Sserafim proves they got talent, charisma, and the audacity to try something different. If you're down for a sonic adventure with catchy tunes and fierce vibes, definitely give it a listen! But maybe don't expect a perfectly polished masterpiece just yet.
