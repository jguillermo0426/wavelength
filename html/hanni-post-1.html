The Perfect Red Velvet

Red Velvet

reviewed by hannipham on January 26, 2024

Album

K-Pop

Contemporary R&B

SUCH A BOP

Omg Reveluvs, did y'all snag "The Perfect Red Velvet" yet?! This album is NO JOKE. Like, I wasn't even a hardcore stan before, but this album SLAPS so hard.

First off, the whole vibe is SOOO cohesive. It's like dark, sultry Red Velvet on a whole new level, and honestly, I'm here for it. Every song feels like it belongs, you know? No skips, just straight bops from start to finish.

And can we talk about the VOCALS?! Wendy's belting in "Peek-A-Boo" is ICONIC, and Joy's voice is smooth as butter in "Look." The harmonies are on point too, giving each song so much depth.

Speaking of "Peek-A-Boo," that song is an INSTANT ADDICTION. Seriously, it's been stuck in my head for days (not complaining!). And "Bad Boy"? Don't even get me started. That smooth, sexy vibe with the killer bassline? Red Velvet is OWNing the "velvet" concept, and I'm LIVING for it.

Now excuse me while I go blast "Peek-A-Boo" on repeat for the 100th time.
